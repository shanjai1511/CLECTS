Safety Protocols

Safety Precautions:

  • Always wear appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE), including lab coats, safety goggles, and gloves.
  • Keep the workspace clean and organized to avoid accidents and spills.
  • Know the location and proper use of emergency equipment, such as eyewash stations and fire extinguishers.
  • Never eat, drink, or apply cosmetics in the lab.
  • Read and understand all experiment instructions before starting.

Steps to Follow:

  1. Perform experiments only under the supervision of a qualified lab instructor.
  2. Always use chemical substances in the designated area, and never mix unknown substances.
  3. Handle all chemicals with care and avoid inhaling fumes directly.
  4. If you spill any chemicals, immediately alert your instructor and follow the proper cleanup procedures.
  5. When heating substances, use a fume hood if possible, and never leave the heating source unattended.
  6. Dispose of all chemical waste properly in designated containers.
  7. Wash your hands thoroughly after each lab session.
  8. If you have any allergies or medical conditions, inform your lab instructor beforehand.
  9. Never perform unauthorized experiments or modifications to the lab equipment.
