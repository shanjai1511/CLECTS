Safety Precautions:

  • Always wear appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE), including lab coats, safety goggles, and gloves.
  • Always wear appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE), including lab coats, safety goggles, and gloves.
  • Keep the workspace clean and organized to avoid accidents and spills.
  • Know the location and proper use of emergency equipment, such as eyewash stations and fire extinguishers.
  • Never eat, drink, or apply cosmetics in the lab.
  • Read and understand all experiment instructions before starting.

Steps to Follow:

  • Perform experiments only under the supervision of a qualified lab instructor.
  • Always use chemical substances in the designated area, and never mix unknown substances.
  • Handle all chemicals with care and avoid inhaling fumes directly.
  • If you spill any chemicals, immediately alert your instructor and follow the proper cleanup procedures.
  • When heating substances, use a fume hood if possible, and never leave the heating source unattended.
  • Dispose of all chemical waste properly in designated containers.
  • Wash your hands thoroughly after each lab session.
  • If you have any allergies or medical conditions, inform your lab instructor beforehand.
  • Never perform unauthorized experiments or modifications to the lab equipment.
